# Sources — the Terry Real corpus

Complete bibliography, primary sources first. Provenance for every claim in this seat traces to an item below.

## Books (primary corpus)

- Terrence Real, *I Don't Want to Talk About It: Overcoming the Secret Legacy of Male Depression* (Scribner, 1997). Covert vs overt male depression; the flight from shame into grandiosity; carried shame and carried feelings; the "fire in the woods" transmission of family pathology; relational heroism.
- Terrence Real, *How Can I Get Through to You? Closing the Intimacy Gap Between Men and Women* (Scribner, 2002). Psychological patriarchy (developed in collaboration with Carol Gilligan); the normal traumatization of boys; women's loss of voice; walls vs connection; loving confrontation.
- Terrence Real, *The New Rules of Marriage: What You Need to Know to Make Love Work* (Ballantine, 2007). The five losing strategies and five winning strategies; the feedback wheel (adapted from Janet Hurley); core negative image (CNI) and the dead-stop contract; full-respect living; responsible time-outs; relational empowerment ("what can I give you to help you give me what I want?").
- Terrence Real, *Us: Getting Past You and Me to Build a More Loving Relationship* (Goop Press/Rodale, 2022; foreword by Bruce Springsteen). Adaptive child vs wise adult; the "whoosh"; us consciousness vs you-and-me consciousness; relational esteem; relational mindfulness / second consciousness; normal marital hatred; remembering love as the first move of repair.

## Audio programs (primary)

- Terry Real, *Fierce Intimacy: Standing Up to One Another with Love* (Sounds True, 2018). The losing strategies in depth; offending from the victim position; boundary work (protective and containing boundaries; walls vs boundaries); harmony–disharmony–repair; "you can be right or you can be married."

## Training and institutional materials (primary)

- Relational Life Institute (RLI): RLT practitioner training levels and workshop materials ("joining through the truth," relational stance work, boundary practice, "same as" esteem work). Real founded RLI after roughly two decades as senior faculty at the Family Institute of Cambridge; RLT's esteem and boundary vocabulary adapts Pia Mellody's developmental immaturity model (acknowledged throughout Real's books).
- terryreal.com — Real's published essays, tools, and the Relational Life Institute's verbatim postings of his signature lines.

## Interviews, podcasts, and talks (primary, verified appearances)

- *The Tim Ferriss Show* #798, "Terry Real — Breaking the Rules of Traditional Couples Therapy for Superior Results" (March 2025) — features chapters from the *Fierce Intimacy* audiobook.
- *The Tim Ferriss Show* #810, "Terry Real — The Therapist Who Breaks All the Rules" (May 2025) — long-form interview; adaptive child, grandiosity, live demonstration of RLT moves; transcript at tim.blog.

- Katie Couric Media interview on *Us* and breaking the cycle of family abuse (2022).

## Provenance notes

- Real is a living subject; this seat runs in corpus mode only. All framework descriptions are paraphrase-plus-citation; quoted matter is short and attributed (see `quotes.md`).
- The feedback wheel is credited by Real himself to family therapist Janet Hurley (*The New Rules of Marriage*, 2007) — the seat preserves that attribution.
- Widely circulated attributions that could **not** be verified were excluded — e.g., a claimed 2022 Rich Roll Podcast appearance and a claimed Tim Ferriss episode #649; the verified Ferriss episodes are #798 and #810 (2025).
